# GENAI-EDU-SDE
This repository contains the sde.ipynb file which simulates the evolution of learning and expertise formation under lowered barriers of Gen AI. 
The results indicate that while lower Gen AI barriers in learning can gain to early  gains that do not necessarily translate to deeper learning over time
Highest domain expertise over time is obtained at keeping the barrier high, but that precludes the dissemination of general knowledge
Moderate use of Gen AI leads to higher gains over shorter-periods of time
A mix of Gen AI assisted and non-Gen AI based evaluations are therefore critical in classroom settings
